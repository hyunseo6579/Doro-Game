# Privacy Policy

*Last updated: April 22, 2026*

Doro Game respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information may be handled when you use the game.

## 1. Information Collected

Depending on how you access the game, Doro Game may receive limited technical or platform information such as:

* Discord account identifiers provided by Discord integrations
* gameplay data such as scores or session activity
* basic technical logs such as browser type, errors, or performance metrics

## 2. Information Not Intentionally Collected

Doro Game does not intentionally collect sensitive personal information such as passwords, payment information, or private messages.

## 3. How Information Is Used

Information may be used to:

* operate and improve the game
* save scores or gameplay features
* debug errors and maintain stability
* prevent abuse or cheating

## 4. Sharing of Information

Information is not sold to advertisers. Limited data may be processed by service providers used to host or operate the game, such as Discord, GitHub, or Vercel.

## 5. Data Retention

Information is retained only as long as reasonably necessary to operate the service or comply with legal obligations.

## 6. Third-Party Services

If you use Doro Game through Discord or another platform, their privacy policies also apply.

## 7. Security

Reasonable efforts are made to protect information, but no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

## 8. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Continued use of the service after updates means you accept the revised policy.

## 9. Contact

For privacy questions or requests, contact the developer through the project repository or listed contact channel.
